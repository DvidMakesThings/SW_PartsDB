CSV Format Requirements

The CSV must have a header row. The importer recognises these columns:

Required: Either a valid DMTUID column, or TT, FF, CC, SS columns (XXX will be auto-assigned).

Core fields: MPN, Value, Manufacturer, Quantity, Location, Description, Datasheet

Template fields: Additional columns matching the template for TT+FF will be stored. Extra columns not in the template are ignored.
